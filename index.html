What is Sad Shayari?

Sad Shayari is a form of poetry that describes feelings of sadness, heartbreak, separation, and emotional struggles. It uses simple but powerful words to connect with the reader’s heart. Unlike normal writing, Shayari combines emotions, imagination, and rhythm to create a deeper impact.

Many people use this form of poetry as a way to release their feelings because sometimes expressing pain through words provides emotional comfort. A few lines of meaningful poetry can describe feelings that may be difficult to explain in a long conversation.
